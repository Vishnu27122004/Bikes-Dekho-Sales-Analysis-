# Bikes Dekho Sales Analysis

An Excel-based business intelligence project that analyzes bike sales revenue and customer demographics to identify high-value segments and support data-driven decisions.

Bikes Dekho Sales Analysis is a comprehensive data analysis project built entirely in Microsoft Excel. The project cleans and models a customer-level bike sales dataset containing demographics, purchase behavior, and revenue fields to uncover key revenue-driving segments and customer patterns.

### Key Features

- **Data Cleaning & Preparation**: Standardized categorical fields and verified data consistency
- **Interactive Dashboard**: Dynamic pivot tables, charts, and slicers for multi-dimensional analysis
- **KPI Metrics**: Total revenue, total customers, total transactions at a glance
- **Customer Segmentation**: Revenue analysis by region, gender, marital status, education, and commute distance
- **Visualization**: Donut charts, bar charts, and trend analysis for actionable insights

The main objective is to analyze bike sales revenue and customer demographics to:

## ğŸ” Key Business Insights

### Regional Performance
- **North America** contributes the highest revenue compared to Europe and Pacific
- Indicates stronger demand and purchasing power in the North American market

### Gender & Marital Status
- **Male customers** show slightly higher revenue contribution
- **Married customers** generate higher revenue than single customers
- Gender-based preferences and purchasing patterns are evident

### Education Level Impact
- **Bachelors degree holders** contribute significantly to revenue
- **Partial College** education level shows strong purchasing behavior
- Graduate degree holders represent a premium segment

### Commute Distance Analysis
- **0-1 miles commute range** generates the highest number of transactions
- Proximity is a critical factor in purchase likelihood
- Short commute distances correlate with bike purchases
